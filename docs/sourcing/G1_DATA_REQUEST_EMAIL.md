# G1 data-request email

**Subject:** Coffee-bed capillary-pressure/saturation or unsaturated-conductivity data

Dear [Name],

I am working on an open-source espresso transport-model registry and am trying to close a
specific constitutive-data gap for a Richards-type wetting model. We are looking for any
measurements on roasted-and-ground coffee beds that resolve water content or saturation
against capillary suction/matric potential, and ideally relative permeability or unsaturated
hydraulic conductivity.

The most useful material would be raw or tabulated (suction, water-content/saturation) points,
or fitted van Genuchten/Brooks–Corey parameters, together with available metadata on grind/PSD,
bulk density or dose/bed geometry, roast and temperature. Wetting and drainage branches and
replicate-level data would be especially valuable.

Front-position-versus-time data alone cannot close this model, so I am specifically asking about
continuous saturation or retention measurements, including unpublished pressure-plate,
centrifuge, NMR/MRI, neutron, or micro-CT measurements.

We would cite the dataset and its provenance prominently and can preserve any reuse conditions
you specify. A negative reply—confirming that such measurements were not made—would also help
avoid duplicating requests.
